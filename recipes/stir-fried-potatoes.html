Stir-Fried Potatoes

Description

Being raised in a chinese household, two dishes stick out in my mind. One is the tried-and-true rice dish, "Fried Rice", and the other is Stir-Fried potato. It is an incredibly simple dish, easy to prepare, that turns a potato into a blend of matchstick shaped sticks that are both savoury and a little sweet. Black rice vinegar and soy sauce blend well together to form a nice umami flavour.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon of cooking oil
  • chili flakes to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon Sichuan peppercorns
  • 3 gloves sliced garlic
  • 1 teaspoon of black rice vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon of soy sauce
  • pinch of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon of brown sugar

Steps

  1. Wash and peel the potato, then cut it into julienne strips.
  2. Rinse the potato strips and drain thoroughly.
  3. Heat up wok on medium heat. Pour in oil, then add dried chili and Sichuan peppercorns. Sizzle till fragrant.
  4. Remove Sichuan peppers then add in garlic. Saute for a minute.
  5. Add potato strips and set the heat to high. Stir-fry for a minute.
  6. Put black rice vinegar,salt, sugar and soy sauce into the wok. Stir-fry for another minute then dish out
  7. Serve with rice
