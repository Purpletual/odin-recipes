Potato Wedges

Description

Potato wedges are a classic side dish that can be enjoyed by all ages. The only thing you need is some potatoes, some seasoning, and a oven. With crispy golden brown exteriors and soft fluffy interiors; potato wedges are truly the best. Recipe from noracooks.

Ingredients

  • 4 russet potatoes or Yukon Gold potatoes
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 425Â°F. Lightly grease large baking pan with oil.
  2. Clean potatoes. Slice potatoes in half lengthwise. Lay the half potatoes on the flat side, then cut in half, lengthwise. Turn over potatoes. Cut through the center to get wedges.
  3. Put wedges into a large bowl. Mix wedges with the rest of the ingredients.
  4. Place coated wedges onto the baking pan. Bake for around 30 minutes, flip them once halfway through. When they are golden brown and fork-tender they are done.
